# DragonBoard Design Principles

Version: 1.0

---

## Mission

DragonBoard is a companion tool for Dragonbane.

It is not intended to replace tabletop play or become a replacement for Foundry VTT.

DragonBoard exists to reduce the Game Master's workload while preserving the tabletop experience.

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Companion Tool

DragonBoard assists the Game Master.

It never replaces the physical game.

---

### 2. Physical First

The following components remain physical:

- Dice
- Initiative Deck
- Treasure Cards
- Adventure Cards
- Improvised Weapon Cards

DragonBoard does not replace these components.

---

### 3. Digital Where It Helps

DragonBoard manages:

- Battle Maps
- Fog of War
- Tokens
- Conditions
- HP Tracking
- Campaign Notes

---

### 4. Tablet First

Player experience is designed for tablets.

---

### 5. GM First

The Game Master always has complete control.

---

### 6. Free Forever

Every technology used by DragonBoard must provide a free tier.

Paid services are optional.

---

### 7. Simplicity Over Features

Simple and reliable is better than feature-rich.

---

### 8. The 10 Second Rule

Any common action should take less than ten seconds.

---

### 9. Dragonbane First

If there is a conflict between generic VTT design and Dragonbane gameplay,

DragonBoard follows Dragonbane.

### 10. One Screen Rule

DragonBoard can't have more than 5 main screen
